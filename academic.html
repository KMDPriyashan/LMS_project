Subject Information

Explore our comprehensive range of subjects with detailed information, modules, and resources to support your academic journey.

Mathematics
The study of numbers, quantities, and shapes. Mathematics explores patterns and relationships, providing foundations for problem-solving and logical reasoning across various fields.
Physics
The scientific study of matter, energy, and their interactions. Physics explores the fundamental principles governing the natural world from subatomic particles to the cosmos.
Computer Science
The study of computers and computational systems. Computer Science covers theory, development, design, and application of software and computer systems.
Biology
The study of living organisms and their interactions with each other and the environment. Biology explores the structure, function, growth, and evolution of living systems.
Chemistry
The study of matter, its properties, and the changes it undergoes. Chemistry explores the composition, structure, properties, and transformations of substances.
Literature
The study of written works with artistic merit. Literature explores various forms of expression including poetry, novels, drama, and essays across different cultures and time periods.
